HOW TO PLAY: When a question appears, type the answer in the answer box and click submit.
Make sure your answer is rounded to the nearest whole integer. (example: 3.1415926535 and 2.7182818284 would both be 3)
Each correct answer is worth 1 point and each wrong answer -1 point.
The more points you have, the stronger your armor and the more likely you are to survive an attack.
When you have acquired enough points, you advance to the next level and face more difficult problems.
Boss battles happen at the end of each level and require you to answer one challenging question.
If you have any difficulty answering a question, check out the help section for the corresponding level.
The game is finished when you reach the final level (7) and defeat the last boss.
Don't forget to leave feedback after playing the game!
